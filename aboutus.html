About Us

What are we all about..

Peterson Miller is an IT solutions firm that builds and manages expert teams in technology. At Peterson Miller, our purpose is to positively impact the lives of our employees, clients, consultants, and the communities in which we operate. Peterson Miller serves as a trusted partner to several small, medium and large companies including Fortune 500 firms enabling businesses to overcome critical business challenges.

We are technology experts who understand that a successful technology implementation depends on much more than simply finding the right combination of hardware and software. It has to start with a clear and strong grasp of each client’s strategy and business requirements. With many decades of combined experience among our leadership and senior consultants, we bring a deep understanding of a wide variety of business environments, which is essential to each assignment we handle for our clients.

What we bring to the table is a mix of operational insight, innovation and best practices. With Premier as your partner, technology no longer has to be a source of chronic frustration. Instead, we will help you find and integrate all the necessary pieces in order to establish a seamless, stable and productive system environment that supports your firm’s current and future success.

We built our business and our reputation by servicing the engineering solutions and talent development needs of our clients. That experience helped make us who we are today—partners with the world’s most forward-thinking organizations, designing, building, operating and automating the technology that powers their businesses. Whether you need innovative technology talent solutions, or you’re looking for opportunities to work with today’s most tech-forward organizations, partner with us—and let us help your business or your career get to the next level.

Our Mission

Our Mission is simple, To power the world of IT Software Workforce by designing, building, attracting & igniting talent in our talent pool while helping and prospering with the communities in which we live in.

We Focus on What Really Matters, PEOPLE

Through innovative workforce solutions we connect great talent with the right cultural fit for our clients. We are in the business of humans and we work hard to build strong relationships with all of them. Whether they are an associate, a client, a partner or a supplier, concentrating on the human element is what makes us all successful.​

Statement of Work (SOW) Management

Our SOW management solution is an extension of our clients’ Procurement and Sourcing functions. Our practitioner-led Services Procurement team and Entry Point Methodology deliver the visibility and compliance our clients need to strategically manage SOW suppliers and project spend

Direct Sourcing Solutions​

Tech Hut Solutions brings together freelancers, independent contractors and self-sourced personnel, incorporating private talent pools that enable you to attract, engage and nurture ‘ready on demand” workers.​

Managed Services Provider (MSP)

Our MSP solutions combine operational scale and excellence with expert guidance to drive continuous value across the talent spectrum, from agency resources to freelancers to project teams and services.​

Workforce Planning and Consulting​

As your trusted partner, nextSource experience and knowledge are extended to you through talent advisory, procurement consultation and transformation planning services.

Payrolling / Employer of Record (EOR)

Our Payrolling (I.E. Employer of Record) solution provides the complete package; we support associates every step of the way from onboarding to reassignment and everything in between. Payrolling is an integral component of the PetersonMillerDirect Sourcing solution.​​
